// ANOMALY DESCRIPTION
       
> DESCRIPTION REQUIRES SC-1 CLEARANCE OR ABOVE
SCP-082 is a large humanoid entity of French origin, standing approximately 2.4 metres in height and bearing a distinctly green skin tone inconsistent with standard human biology. Subject is significantly overweight while simultaneously possessing abnormal muscle mass. Physical examination has been largely inconclusive — muscle density renders standard imaging difficult, and embedded foreign materials including ballistic fragments have been confirmed throughout soft tissue, consistent with prolonged suppression attempts. SCP-082 possesses exceptional physical strength, capable of lifting and projecting standard human personnel with minimal effort. Its jaw structure allows for full consumption of a human subject in a small number of bites.

Under typical conditions, SCP-082 presents as cordial, outgoing, and conversational. It maintains a strong sense of personal dignity and will frequently invite personnel to tea parties or social gatherings within its containment chamber. SCP-082 refers to those it considers associates using food-related nicknames. Subject speaks fluent French and heavily accented English, typically through clenched teeth — SCP-082 only opens its jaw fully to consume food or to sing.

Subject is prone to fabricating personal history and adopts various character personas — referred to internally as "outfits" — through which it behaves in accordance with the assumed role. SCP-082 becomes hostile when it or those it considers associates are insulted, or when referred to by any name other than "Fern" or "Fernand." Hostile escalation is marked by reddening of the eyes. At this stage, subject will attempt to physically assault the offending individual through strikes, throws, or consumption. Attacks are otherwise unpredictable and may occur without discernible motivation.
> FULL BEHAVIOURAL PROFILE REQUIRES SC-2 CLEARANCE OR ABOVE
// SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES
     
> CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES REQUIRE SC-2 CLEARANCE OR ABOVE
SCP-082 is contained within Sector Gamma of Site Neptune in a modified Standard Secure Humanoid Containment Chamber constructed to resemble a medieval French nobleman's estate. The chamber includes a dining room, bedroom, living area, and courtyard. The exterior of the chamber houses an AETV, entry vestibule, and maintenance area.

Subject operates under the belief that it holds a position of French nobility and that Site Neptune constitutes its royal estate. This deception is to be maintained by all interacting staff at all times. Address subject only as "Fern" or "Fernand" — failure to do so constitutes a Class-2 safety incident.

SCP-082 is permitted limited free-roam access at the discretion of on-duty SC-3 or above personnel. Housekeeping functions within the chamber are to be carried out by Class-D personnel only. Security staff are not to engage SCP-082 in direct conversation unless operationally necessary.

// KNOWN PERSONAS
 
> PERSONA RECORDS REQUIRE SC-2 CLEARANCE OR ABOVE
Napoleon Fernaparte — "The General"
SCP-082 presents as a French military general, narrating fabricated accounts of campaigns. Will occasionally recruit Class-D and Foundation personnel into formations and lead them on patrols.
Chef Ferdinand — "The Chef"
SCP-082 presents as a qualified culinary professional. Will gather nearby personnel and escort them to the cafeteria to host what it describes as a cooking demonstration.
Dr. Fernestein — "The Mad Scientist"
SCP-082 presents as a scientist, hosting tests with absurd hypotheses. Will voluntarily spectate legitimate Scientific Department procedures.
Monsieur Fernando — "The Billionaire"
SCP-082 presents as an arrogant aristocrat. Will host tea parties exclusively for personnel it deems "of sufficient status" — typically SC-3 and above. Will dismiss lower-ranked personnel.
The Great Fernando — "The Wizard"
SCP-082 presents as a powerful sorcerer, performing elaborate rituals it claims to be genuine magic. Will host "sorcery shows" for gathered personnel.
Detective Ferlock — "The Detective"
SCP-082 presents as a renowned investigator. Will identify "cases" among personnel — thievery, trespassing, rule violations — and either hand suspects to security or resolve them personally.
Fernie — "The Tourist"
SCP-082 presents as a visiting civilian and will conduct impromptu tours of Site Neptune, renaming areas. Known aliases include: Sector Gamma → "Le Restaurant Des Oranges", Sector Epsilon → "The Zoo".
Captain Fern — "The Pirate"
SCP-082 presents as a sea captain in search of treasure. Will attempt to recruit a crew from available personnel for what it describes as maritime adventures.
Sheriff Fern — "The Sheriff"
SCP-082 presents as a frontier lawman. Refers to Site Neptune as "his town" and Class-D personnel as "outlaws." Makes frequent use of Western vernacular and greatly exaggerates personal accounts.
Santa Fern — "Father Christmas"
SCP-082 presents as Father Christmas. Will attempt to recruit personnel as elves. Seasonal occurrence — typically observed during December.
Fernstein — "Frankenstein's Monster"
SCP-082 claims to be a creation of SCP-049, cured and given life. Behaviour largely mirrors standard SCP-082 conduct with added references to its alleged "creation." NOTE: This persona appears to reflect genuine belief on SCP-082's part.
// BREACH PROTOCOL & RECONTAINMENT — SC-3 RESTRICTED
BREACH PROTOCOL

In the event SCP-082 exits its containment chamber without authorisation, the following applies:

Standard weapons are largely ineffective against SCP-082 and direct confrontation is to be avoided. Mobile Task Force units are to locate subject and reestablish the French nobility deception immediately. SCP-082 is to be guided back toward its containment chamber under the premise of returning to its royal estate.

Under no circumstances is SCP-082 to be referred to by any name other than "Fern" or "Fernand" during a breach event. Personnel encountering a hostile SCP-082 — identifiable by red eye colouration — must maintain a minimum distance and await MTF arrival. Do not attempt recontainment alone.

RECONTAINMENT PROTOCOL

Recontainment is to be led by on-duty Gamma-7 units. Upon successful de-escalation, SCP-082 is to be escorted back to its chamber. The deception narrative should be reinforced — reference the estate, its status, and suggest that the excursion was a sanctioned visit. A post-breach welfare check by a Junior Researcher or above is recommended within 24 hours to assess subject's emotional state and stability.
